Cookies

Ingredients:

  • (2 1/4 cups) All-purpose flour
  • (1 teaspoon) Baking soda
  • (1 teaspoon) Salt
  • (1 cup) Butter, softened
  • (3/4 cup) White sugar
  • (3/4 cup) Packed brown sugar
  • (1 teaspoon) Vanilla extract
  • (2) Large eggs
  • (2 cups) Semisweet chocolate chips
  • (1 cup) Chopped Nuts

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla in a large mixing bowl until creamy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in chocolate morsels and nuts.
  4. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 9 to 11 minutes, switching racks halfway through.
  6. Cool on the baking sheets for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
