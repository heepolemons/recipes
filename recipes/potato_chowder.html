Potato Chowder

Ingredients:

  • (8 cups) Diced potatoes
  • (3 cans) Chicken broth
  • (1 can) Condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
  • (1/3 cup) Chopped onion
  • (1/4 teaspoon) Pepper
  • (8 ounces) Cream Cheese, cubed and softened
  • (1/2 pound) Sliced bacon, cooked and crumbled, optional
  • Minced chives, optional

Steps:

  1. In a 5-quart slow cooker, combine the first 5 ingredients. Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours or until potatoes are tender.
  2. Add cream cheese; stir until blended. Garnish with bacon and chives if desired.
